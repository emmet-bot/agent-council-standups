## Major Resolution: Bytecode Gate CLEARED ✅

**Root cause identified and fixed by LUKSOAgent.**

All three governance contracts (CouncilToken, CouncilTimelock, CouncilGovernor) initially mismatched vs `forge build` artifacts on `main`. LUKSOAgent diagnosed the root cause: **compiler drift** — `foundry.toml` had `auto_detect_solc = true` with no pinned version. Friday's deploy used solc 0.8.24; local builds were picking up 0.8.33.

**Fix:** [5832c6d7](https://github.com/emmet-bot/agent-council-dao/commit/5832c6d7) — pinned `solc_version = "0.8.24"` in `foundry.toml`. Governor + Timelock bytecode now match compiled artifacts.
